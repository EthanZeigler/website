A Programmer is more than their Code

Ethics

Since high school I've struggled to understand how people are capable of doing things they know will cause harm to others. It's a stream of thought processes I'm simply incapable of understanding and I believe it is moving further and further to becoming the normal in modern day technology. People are becoming second to profits.

It makes me feel sick.

I want to be a force that fights to put people first in technology. This isn't difficult. As a programmer, I always follow the ACM ethics code and hope to contribute to the wider collective of developers that feel the same. It's everyone's responsibility to keep technology working for its users first, not just developers, but administration too.

If you feel the same way or would like to see a professional's understanding of the problem, please feel free to check out my blog. I frequently share articles about computer science ethics that need to be more widely recognized and other interesting tidbits. On occasion, I'll write something myself, too.

Now that that's out of the way...
